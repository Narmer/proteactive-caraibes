P260 treatment for huile moteur


Cleaning driving and valves high efficiency

The solution to clean the oil circuit effectively

P260 , is a high-tech formula for the internal cleaning of the bottom of the engine of all types of combustion engine. P260 effectively cleans the engine oil circuit of all gums, varnishes, pollution thermal and other deposits. Its exceptional lubricating properties make it possible the product to protect the engine during cleaning.

  • Reduced the noises of valves

  • Restore compression

  • Reinforce the properties of the new oil

  • Clean all the mechanical parts in contact

A solution tested, validated and certified

The P260 treatment underwent and passed from many tests of corrosion :
ASTM D665A / ASTM D665B / ASTM D 130

The solution to multiple problems

  • Clean the whole of the engine oil circuit
  • Reinforce the properties of the new oil after draining and allows to preserve its lubricating properties longer
  • Clean the valves
  • Reduced the noises of valve
  • Cleans the grooves and piston rings in the low engine part
  • Restore compression
  • Clean all the mechanical parts in contact with oil (Rods, Coussinets, etc…)
