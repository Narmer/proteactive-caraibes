Treatment PROTEA P350 Diesel and oil circuit cleaning Engine


The Company

UNIFIPECHE, a Guyanese maritime company made up of 24 fishing boats each consuming 48,000 liters of diesel per month. Each boat is equipped with 2 engines :
- 1 engine of the CUMMINS type K19 and a power of 425 HP at 1850 rpm.
- 1 engine running the compressor 24 hrs (for the freezer) of the brand ISUZU with a power of 65 HP.

They testify

  • Mr. Stanley WATKINS

After the use of PROTEA P350 and P260, we found :
- A considerable increase in the torque of our motors.
- An extended life of our engines (cylinder head, valve, etc.).
- Better engine efficiency.
- A faster start-up of our engines.
- A very clean oil after 2 hours of engine operation.
- An oil pressure of 73 PSI on the engine CUMMINS instead of 60 PSI before (hot engine rotating in both cases at 900 rpm.

Mr Stanley WATKINS Wharf head of UNIFIPÊCHE

General Info

  • Client:UNIFIPÊCHE
  • Kind:Boats
  • Place:Guyana

The solution

Proteactive Caraïbes recommends treatment with the PROTEA P350 Diesel and a cleaning of the engine oil circuit at the PROTEA P260.

The procedure

  1. Refilling PROTEA P350 tanks
  2. Filling PROTEA P350 with diesel filters
  3. P260 in the propulsion engine after reaching the correct engine temperature
  4. Draining for 1h30 with the P260 and filling of oil in the engine
  5. Checking the oil level before starting the engine
  6. Oil check on the gauge after 2 hours of operation

Benefits for the customer

  • Significant increase in engine torque,
  • Extended engine life (cylinder head, valve, etc.),
  • Better engine output,
  • Faster starting of engines,
  • The oil remains clean after 2 hours of engine operation.
  • Increased oil pressure (73 PSI instead of 60 PSI.
