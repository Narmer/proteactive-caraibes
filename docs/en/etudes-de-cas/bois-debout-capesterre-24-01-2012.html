Testing of PROTEA P2 treatments to the company Bois Debout


The Company

Company Bois Debout SA has agreed to test our products with a view to improving the performance of one of their vehicles.

We want to process the fuel supply chain (diesel filter and tank) and process the engine oil.

General Info

  • Date:24 January 2012
  • Client:Bois Debout SA
  • Kind:Jeep Mercedes
  • Place:Guadeloupe, Capesterre

The solution

Proteactive Caraïbes recommends a diesel treatment of the PROTEA P550 DIESEL for the fuel supply chain (diesel filter and tank) and a treatment of engine oil to the PROTEA P260 FLUSHING.

The procedure

  1. Checking the quality of the diesel oil in the filter and the level of contamination in the strainer.
  2. Use 2 vials of PROTEA P550 of 460 ml, the first in the new diesel filter and the 2nd in the tank.
  3. Installation of 600 ml FLUSHING P260 in the front oil pan of fill up.
  4. Departure for 1 hour of road, tour visit exploitation then direction the Falls of the Carbet.
  5. Back to the site and emptying,
  6. Filling with new oil,
  7. Checking the oil 3 days after,
  8. Last oil check 10 days later.

Benefits for the customer

Several observations were made. Better motor start-up and no knocking out, the engine has become more flexible, no need to downshift in the climbs, oil remains liquid and the crankcase clean after 24 kms traveled

  • Removal of engine rattling,
  • Better engine performance,
  • Clean strainer, removal of impurities,
  • The oil is like new after 346 kms traveled since the test.
