Test of the PROTEA P2 in the EDF workshop in Dégrad des Cannes


The problems

The injection parts are covered with scale and require cleaning.

General Info

  • Date:20 June 2013
  • Client:Dégrad des Cannes | EDF
  • Place:Guyane

The solution

Proteactive Caraïbes recommends to treat injection parts to the PROTEA P550 Diesel by immersion.

The procedure

  1. Immersion of the injection parts in the pre-filled tank to the PROTEA P550 Diesel.
  2. The coated injections remain in the PROTEA P550 for 48 hours.
  3. The parts are removed and placed in a drying box.

Benefits for the customer

The customer obtains a very good result, equivalent to the use of its usual solvent Clean Sol.
The use of the PROTEA P2 P550 Diesel is very effective for static cleaning (immersion), because it allows duplication after a simple filtration and can be reused as a biocidal treatment.
