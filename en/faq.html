General questions

PROTEA P2, what is it ?

PROTEA P2 is a set of treatments for hydrocarbons, the range of products includes :

  • Treatment for Diesel engines, ovens and boilers, the PROTEA P550 (diesel & fuel oil),
  • Treatment for gasoline engines, the PROTEA P231,
  • Treatment for motor oil, the PROTEA P260,
  • Un traitement pour les cuves de stockage, le Stock Cleaner (réservé pour les professionnels).
Why use PROTEA P2 ?

Simple, if you answer Yes to at least one of the following questions, then PROTEA P2 is the answer to your problem.

  • You have problems of rattling of the engine?
  • You have problems of starting the engine?
  • You have problems when engine slow down?
  • You have water in your tank or your storage tank fuel?
  • You have a proliferation of microorganisms (bacteria, yeasts, mould and fungi) in your reservoir or your tank?
  • You regularly change your filters?
  • You want to reduce your fuel consumption?
  • Have strong fumes of exhaust smoke?
  • You want to succeed your anti-pollution technical control?
  • You have valves EGR problems?
  • You have problems of lubrication in the injection system (injectors, injection, high valve pump)?
  • You have problems of lubrication in the supply circuit?
  • Or, more generally, you have problems of lubrication in the engine (combustion chambers, senior of cylinders)?
  • You want to protect the parts of the engine against rust and erosion?
  • Your vehicle does not meet the acceleration?
  • Your fuel is loaded into molecules of sulfur?
  • Your injectors are removed?
  • Your engine emits high temperatures, because of the friction?
HOW DOES PROTEA P2 WORK?

Our treatments simply work by being mixed with the fuel. It is required to pour the product before the filling of the tank to get an homogeneous mixture.

HOW TO USE PROTEA P2?

You just have to pour the dose of PROTEA P2 in the tank or the reservoir of storage. We recommend to pour P2 PROTEA before refuelling to speed up the action of the product.

WHAT DOSAGE OF PROTEA P2 MAY I USE?

Advise : A slight overdose is preferable to an under dosage.

  • For Diesel engines (PROTEA P550):
  • For your first use, you are in the curative dosage of 1 liter of PROTEA P550 for 150 litres of Diesel.
  • After the first use, you are in the preventive dosage of 1 liter ofPROTEA P2 for 320 liters of diesel (1/3 full) or continuous (1 l to 1000 l of diesel).
  • You can check the dosage of Diesel treatment table on the product page.
  • For petrol (PROTEA P231) engines:
  • PROTEA P231 treatment is only used as curative dosage of 1 liter of PROTEA P231 for 320 liters of gasoline.
  • You can check the dosage of the petrol treatment table on the product page.
  • For oils (PROTEA P260):
  • The dosage is 1 liter of PROTEA P260 for 10 liters of oil.

Our treatments are persistent, they permanently protect the engines, tanks, vats, etc.

Important : It is not necessary to use PROTEA P2 to each full of fuel, you can treat every 3 fulls.

WHAT IS THE CETANE INDEX ?

There are 2 cetane index, the first one is measured and is used to assess the ability to self-ignition of a diesel and the second one is calculated from data analytical. Cetane index allows to value more or less quality of a diesel, example, the more the cetane index is low the more he approaches the noncombustibility through phases such as the creation of unburned (pollutant emissions).

I HAVE WATER IN THE TANK OF MY VEHICLE, WHAT TO DO ?

Our treatment removes water from fuel. How? The water in the bottom of the tank (foot of water) from the condensation and representing up to 8% of the total volume of the tank will be atomized and reduced to 0, 5μ. The water molecules will be coated by potassium molecules brought by our treatments, which will facilitate their transport to combustion chamber.
